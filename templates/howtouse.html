How to Use

  • 1. Input your desired year: The range of year shoud be Within 1983-2020
  • 2. Input your desired mileage: The range of mileage shoud be Within 13-42
  • 3. Input your desired Maxpower: The range of Maxpower shoud be Within 100-400

Inputs for Prediction:

Within all of the algorithms, you'll be prompted to enter three attributes of the car for which you wish to predict the price:

  • Year: The production year of the car.
  • Max_Power: The maximum power output of the car.
  • Mileage: The fuel consumption of the car.

After entering these details, click on the "Predict" button. Shortly after, the predicted price and its explanation of the car will be displayed.
