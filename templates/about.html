About the Car Prediction Application

Our Car Prediction Application leverages the power of machine learning to estimate car attributes based on provided features. We utilize advanced algorithms including Multinomial Logistic Regression, Ridge Logistic Regression, and Gaussian Naive Bayes to ensure our predictions are accurate and reliable.

Data Behind The App

The application is trained on a rich dataset of car listings collected from various sources. This data encompasses diverse car features which our models use to make predictions.

Algorithms

  • Multinomial Logistic Regression: A classification algorithm suitable for multi-class categorization.
  • Ridge Logistic Regression: A variant of logistic regression with added regularization for better generalization.
  • Gaussian Naive Bayes: A probabilistic algorithm based on Bayes' theorem, it's simple and fast.
